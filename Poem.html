Happy Teachers Day!


Dearest Teachers,
You greet us every morning with a warm smile on your face
Even though sometimes finishing work can become a race
You teach us with humour and patience
And answer all our questions
You treat every one of us with the same amount of dedication
You work hard everyday
Make our fears go away
You teach us the difference between wrong and right
You teach us to be helpful, caring and wise
You help us understand that no matter how dark the night
The sun will always rise
In our lives you make a big impact
We must remember that
For the dawn of each author, each doctor or king
Begins with a teacher and the wisdom they bring
Love and a ton of gratitude,

Vani Sharma + The Rest of Grade 7E (2021-2022)








Thank You for Everything!
